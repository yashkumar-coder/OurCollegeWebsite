Information about MIET

Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology (MIET) was founded in 1997 and is a part of MIET group.
It is managed by City Educational and Social Welfare Society, Meerut. The Institute is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure and teaching methodology. It is one of the top engineering colleges of Uttar Pradesh.
MIET is a private engineering institute affiliated to AKTU, Lucknow and approved by AICTE.
It is accredited by NBA and NAAC. NAAC has awarded 'B++' Grade to MIET.
Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology is one of the oldest self-financed engineering colleges in Northern India

MIET Admission Process

MIET offers BTech, BVoc, BPharm, MTech, MPharm, MBA and MCA programmes. The Institute offers all these courses in full-time mode. Interested candidates can apply for admission to these courses online. Mentioned below are MIET admission process highlights: 

    *  Admission to MIET BTech programme is based on JEE Main or UPSEE rank

    *  Candidates are selected for BPharm programme based on merit

    *  A valid score in GATE score is required for admission to MIET MTech programme

    *  MPharm admission is based on GPAT score

    *  For admission to MIET MCA, a valid score in UPSEE is required

    *  Admission to MBA programme is based on CAT, MAT or UPSEE score

    *  NRI students can also apply for BTech programme

The training and placement cell of MIET is responsible for maintaining the database of prospective recruiters and help students to enhance their communication and problem-solving skills. Top companies such as ICICI Bank, Accenture, L&T, TCS and Infosys visit the campus for recruitment every year.
